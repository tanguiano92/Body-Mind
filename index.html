Make Your Own Ink Blots

The connection of psychology and the mind's ability to create it's
own reality and images. This piece is a comment on the brains ability
to create images and scenes out of nonsense and how doctors would use
these form of timed psychological tests to determine the mental well-being
of humans. Are you crazy? Or simply imaginitive?
